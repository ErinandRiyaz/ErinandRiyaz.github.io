Wedding Day Details

The Ceremony 

Our ceremony will take place at 2:00PM at the Canmore Ranch. There will be yard games and light refreshments set up after the ceremony for those that want to have some fun before heading to the reception. Free parking is available at the ranch. It is only a 10 minute walk from the reception for those who do not want to drive.  
Address: 104 Bow Valley Trail, Canmore, AB T1W 3B7

Reception

Now that all the "I do's" are "I done's", join us for good food, great music, and bad dancing! The reception will take place at Cornerstore Theatre. Cocktails will start at 6:00PM and dinner at 7:00PM.  Doors close at 2:00 AM.
Address: 125 Kananaskis Way, Canmore, AB T1W 2X2

 Extra Details

There will be a toonie bar open for the evening. Many of the hotels are walking distance from the reception, but if you choose to drive, please no drinking and driving!
